About the Festival

Yugadi Sambrahma

ಯುಗಾದಿ ಸಂಭ್ರಮ • ಹೊಸ ವರ್ಷ

Yugadi marks the beginning of the new year in the Hindu lunisolar calendar, celebrated with great fervor across Karnataka. Families gather to savor the iconic Bevu-Bella (neem and jaggery), symbolizing life's blend of bitter and sweet. The day features Panchanga Shravana, rangoli, cultural performances, and a grand festive feast.
