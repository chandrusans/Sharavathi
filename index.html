About the Festival

Yugadi / Ugadi

ಯುಗಾದಿ ಹಬ್ಬ • ಹೊಸ ವರ್ಷ

Yugadi marks the beginning of the new year in the Hindu lunisolar calendar, celebrated with great fervor across Karnataka. Families gather to savor the iconic Bevu-Bella (neem and jaggery), symbolizing life's blend of bitter and sweet. The day features Panchanga Shravana, rangoli, cultural performances, and a grand festive feast.

Day 2 Tradition

Badoota

ಬಾಡೂಟ

Badoota is a beloved tradition observed the day after Yugadi in many Karnataka households. After the sacred vegetarian observance, families and friends come together for a hearty non-vegetarian feast featuring classic Karnataka dishes. It celebrates community, togetherness, and the rich culinary heritage of Karnataka.
